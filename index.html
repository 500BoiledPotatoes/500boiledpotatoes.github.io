About

I am a postgraduate student at Australian National University, previously at Beijing University of Technology (BJUT) and University College Dublin, where I graduated with First Class Honours.

My work sits at the intersection of computer architecture and systems software. I care about how programs map onto hardware — from cache hierarchies and memory bandwidth to inter-node communication — and how to extract maximum throughput from modern parallel architectures.

More recently I have been exploring LLM safety research, contributing to open-source tooling for probing language model robustness.
